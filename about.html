Our Story

Committed to Excellence in Global Logistics

Proventurax International Logistics was founded in 2024 by two friends with a shared commitment to operational excellence and high-quality customer service. With more than 25 years of combined experience in the logistics industry, we are headquartered in Bangalore, India, and serve clients worldwide with complete import and export solutions.

We place a strong emphasis on supporting the industrial and manufacturing hubs of South India, including Chennai, Coimbatore, Hosur, Mysuru, and Hyderabad—regions known for their thriving automotive, textile, aerospace, and technology sectors.

Through our trusted global partner network, we provide comprehensive freight forwarding services that ensure reliable, efficient, and seamless connections between South India and the world.

As we look ahead, Proventurax remains committed to embracing innovation, sustainability, and digital solutions to deliver smarter logistics and create lasting value for our clients across the globe.
