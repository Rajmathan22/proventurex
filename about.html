About Proventurax

Proventurax International Logistics was founded in 2024 by two friends with a shared vision for operational excellence and customer service. Headquartered in Bangalore, we bring over 25 years of combined experience to deliver complete import and export solutions for clients worldwide.

Connecting South India to the World

We specialize in supporting the industrial and manufacturing hubs of Chennai, Coimbatore, Hosur, Mysuru, and Hyderabad—regions known for their thriving automotive, textile, aerospace, and technology sectors. Our trusted global partner network ensures seamless, reliable, and efficient freight forwarding.

Driven by Innovation & Sustainability

We embrace digital solutions and sustainable practices to deliver smarter logistics and lasting value for our clients. Our commitment to innovation keeps us at the forefront of the industry.
